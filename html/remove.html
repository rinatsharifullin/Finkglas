Tattoo removal

So you decided that you want to remove your beloved tattoo. We can help you with it. In our shop we use Laser method of tattoo removal. Tattoos consist of thousands of particles of tattoo ink suspended in the skin. The normal human immune system typically removes small foreign particles from the skin. Tattoo ink particles are too big to be removed by this system and are thus considered permanent. The use of lasers helps to break up these particles into smaller pieces that can be removed by the immune system. Complete removal of a tattoo is usually not accomplished in one laser treatment session. Depending on the tattoo design, size and colour, it may take 1-10 laser sessions to remove the ink. Some colours of ink are harder to remove than others and it may not be possible to completely remove them. There should be a six-week time period between each laser session to allow the wounds to heal and the body to absorb as much of the ink as is possible.
