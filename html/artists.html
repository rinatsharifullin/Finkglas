Stevo

Stevo, owner of Finkglas Tattoo was born and bred in Finglas. He has over 20 years of experience as a tattoo artist, travelling the world, working in Los Angeles, San Francisco, Sydney, Puerto Rico, and London to name but a few. He is well known for his precision in rendering colourful tattoos.He specialises in Traditional and Realistic. Join him for a double espresso macchiato from his brand new espresso machine and chat!

Seanie worked as a graphic designer for 5 years before deciding to begin his journey as a tattoo artist. He is very passionate about creating clean, bold and precise designs. He is a man of few words until you get him talking about tattoo styles, colours, ink-machines, football, politics, the weather,… He specialises in Traditional, Neo-Traditional and Celtic. Come in for a consultation if you have a few hours to spare!!

Simona

Born in Guadalajara, Simona spent much of her childhood painting with her mother who was a renowned Japanese painter. She began using skin as her canvas some years later. She has worked as a tattoo artist in Mexico City, Los Angeles, Paris, and London. She fell in love with our resident globe trotter Stevo in London a few years back. She loves a challenge clearly (!), so come in and chat with her if you have an idea that you’d like her to be bring to reality or you’re looking for inspiration or a simple exploration about what’s possible. She specialises in Japanese and New School.

Rodolfo, originally from Venice Beach, California, came to visit Ireland after falling in love with the tv show Father Ted. Five years later, he now calls Dublin his home. You might not think it, but Rodolfo is the resident agony aunt and can be regularly seen comforting Stevo after another lost battle in love! He is a really talented artist and can make any idea come to life. He has a huge variety of styles in his arsenal, specialising in Tribal and Chicano. Come in with your idea for a chat or just come for a hug! You won’t be disappointed!
